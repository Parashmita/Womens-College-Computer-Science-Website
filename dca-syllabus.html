Diploma in Computer Application (DCA)

Duration: 1 Year / 144 Hours

Programme Outcome

The curriculum imparts theoretical and practical knowledge in MS Windows, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, RDBMS (Access), Internet, Accounting, and Tally with GST. It prepares students for higher studies or job roles like Data Entry Operator, Office Assistant, etc.

Certificate Course in LibreOffice

Offered by Department of Computer Science in collaboration with IIT Bombay

Detailed Syllabus

Unit Topic Theory (Hours) Practical (Hours)
I LO Writer: Creating, saving and editing documents, formatting text, headers, footers, WordArt, bookmarks, tables, mail merge, printing. 3 6
II LO Calc: Creating worksheets, entering data, formatting cells, formulas, charts, sorting/filtering, using wizards and legends. 4 10
III LO Impress: Slide creation, formatting text and objects, transitions, custom animations, printing slides, working with slide layouts. 3 4
Total 10 20
Assessment: Term-end theory and practical examination.

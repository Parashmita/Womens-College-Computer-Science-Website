About the Department

Established in 2003, the Department of Computer Science offers Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA) and Postgraduate Diploma in Computer Application (PGDCA) as its main programs. In addition, it provides IT-related courses for BA, B.Com, and higher secondary students, along with short-term Career Oriented Courses (COC). The curriculum blends theory and application, focusing on emerging IT fields and problem-solving skills to prepare students for real-world challenges.

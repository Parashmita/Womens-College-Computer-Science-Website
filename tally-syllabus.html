Certificate Course in Tally

Duration: 3 Months / 36 Hours

Programme Outcome

This course is designed to impart knowledge of financial accounting using the Tally ERP 9 software. It covers Accounting Basics, Voucher Entry, Inventory Management, and Report Generation. Upon completion, students will be capable of working as Tally Data Entry Operators with skills in GST compliance.

Detailed Syllabus

Unit Topic Theory Practical
I Basics of Accounting: Journal, Ledger, Trial Balance, Final Accounts, Accounting Principles 5 –
II Fundamentals of Tally: Introduction, Company Features, Configuration 1 2
III Accounts Information: Ledgers, Groups in Tally, Alter/Delete, Shortcut Keys 1 3
IV Inventory Information: Stock Groups, Items, Godown, Stock Summary 2 7
V Recording Day-to-Day Transactions: Vouchers, Invoicing, Voucher Entry 2 10
VI Generating Reports: Day Book, Financial Statements, Profit & Loss, Balance Sheet 1 2
Total 12 24

  • 102 – Programming with C (Theory & Practical)

  • 104 – Data Communication and Computer Network (Theory & Practical)
  • 105 – Project I (System Analysis, SDLC, Minor Project)

Semester 2

  • 201 – Introduction to Multimedia (Theory & Practical)
  • 202 – Desktop Publishing (Theory & Practical)
  • 203 – Internet & Web Technology (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP)
  • 204 – Mobile Technology (Android, iOS, App Dev Tools)
  • 205 – Project II (Final Project + Seminar + Viva)

About the Computer Science Department

The Computer Science Department at Women's College Tinsukia is committed to providing quality education in the field of technology, equipping students with the skills needed to excel in the digital era. Our department offers two major programs: Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) and Post Graduate Diploma in Computer Applications (PGDCA), catering to both undergraduate and postgraduate students.

With a comprehensive curriculum, experienced faculty, and state-of-the-art facilities, we focus on programming, web development, data science, networking, and emerging technologies like AI and cybersecurity. Our department emphasizes practical learning, industry exposure, and career-oriented training, ensuring that students are well-prepared for the ever-evolving IT industry.
